    \equiv x \in A \vee x \in (B \cap C)
    \equiv x \in A \vee (x \in B \wedge x \in C)
    \equiv (x \in A \vee x \in B) \wedge (x \in A \vee x \in C)
    \equiv x \in (A \cup B) \cap (A \cup C)$
